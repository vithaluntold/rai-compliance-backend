Consolidated Financial Statements of Phoenix Group PLC

For the year ended December 31, 2024

DIRECTORS' REPORT

Phoenix Group PLC (incorporated in England and Wales)

Company Registration Number: 1234567

The Group is engaged in various businesses primarily the development, sales, investment, construction, leasing, management and associated services for real estate. In addition, the Group is also engaged in development, construction, management and operation of hotels, schools, marinas, restaurants, beach clubs and golf courses.

The Group operates primarily in the United Arab Emirates with subsidiaries incorporated in England and Wales.

These consolidated financial statements comprise the financial statements of Phoenix Group PLC and its subsidiaries for the year ended December 31, 2024.
